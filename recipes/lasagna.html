Lasagna

INGREDIENTS

  • ground beef
  • onion and garlic
  • marinara sauce
  • ricotta cheese
  • egg
  • Parmesan and mozzarella cheese
  • lasagna noodles

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Cook ground beef with onion and garlic, add marinara sauce.
  2. Mix ricotta with egg and Parmesan cheese.
  3. Boil noodles or use oven-ready ones.
  4. Layer sauce, noodles, cheese mix, and mozzarella in a baking dish.
  5. Bake at 375Â°F for 45 minutes; let rest before serving.
