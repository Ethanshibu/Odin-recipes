Brownie

INGREDIENTS

  • butter
  • chocolate/cocoa powder
  • sugar
  • eggs
  • vanilla extract
  • flour
  • salt

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Melt butter and chocolate together, let cool slightly.
  2. Stir in sugar, eggs, and vanilla extract.
  3. Mix in flour, cocoa powder, and a pinch of salt.
  4. Pour batter into a greased or lined 8x8" pan.
  5. Bake at 350°F for 20–25 minutes, cool before cutting.
